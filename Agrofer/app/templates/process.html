Our Process

From salon floors to fertile fields, here’s how we transform human hair into sustainable fertilizers.

1. Hair Collection

We partner with over 500 salons across Pakistan to collect human hair, diverting tons of waste from landfills.

Our collection network ensures a steady, sustainable supply while supporting local businesses.

2. Cleaning & Processing

After collecting the hair, we wash it thoroughly to remove dust and other unwanted materials. Then, we dry and treat it using a safe process that helps release the nutrients inside.

This clean and processed hair becomes the key ingredient for our natural liquid fertilizer.

3. Fertilizer Production

The processed hair is blended with natural additives like alkaline solutions and minerals to create high-quality fertilizers.

Each batch is tested to ensure optimal nutrient content for various crops.

4. Distribution

Once our liquid fertilizer is ready, we carefully package it and deliver it to farmers, nurseries, and gardening enthusiasts.

We aim to make AGROFER easily accessible, whether through local markets, direct orders, or eco-friendly delivery partners—bringing green solutions straight to the soil.

Benefits of Our Process

Eco-Friendly

Reduces waste and carbon footprint.

Nutrient-Rich

High nitrogen content boosts soil health.

Scalable

Supports farmers of all sizes.
