About Agrofer

Our Story

It all started with a simple question: What if waste could grow life? At AGROFER, we looked at the piles of discarded human and animal hair — a material rich in nutrients yet thrown away every day — and saw untapped potential. Inspired by nature’s ability to recycle everything, we set out to create something powerful, natural, and sustainable.

After months of experimentation and research, we developed a process to transform hair into a nutrient-rich liquid fertilizer, full of essential elements. Today, we’re proud to offer farmers and gardeners a sustainable alternative to chemical fertilizers, proving that even waste can grow a better future..
